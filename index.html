About Me

Welcome to my website! I'm Bhupendra Bhardwaj, a highly experienced lawyer practicing primarily in the District and Session Court, Jaipur Metro I-II & District, State /District Consumer Forum, and High Court at Jaipur Bench. With over 26 years of experience in the legal field, I bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table.

Apart from holding a degree in Law (LL.B.), I've also obtained a post-graduate degree in Labor Law (LL.M) and a PG Diploma in Criminal Law. Over the years, I've built a strong reputation for providing sound legal advice and effective representation to my clients.

I'm proud to be enlisted with the official panel of corporations, including Rajasthan Housing Board, RIICO LTD., Bhartia AXA General Insurance, Fairdeal Technology Pvt. Ltd., Marie Stopes India, Raheja QBE General Insurance, and Sterling Urban Co-operative Bank Ltd. If you're in need of legal assistance in any of the areas where I practice, please don't hesitate to reach out to me.
